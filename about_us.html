Hey Friends!

I am Isabelle Jemma Ruth Robinson and it appears that you have landed on my blog! That's so exciting, welcome! I write to make sense of the world and I rant about a lot of things. Maybe you will connect to something that I say.

I am a digital journalist. I am a computer programmer. I am a teacher. I am a world traveler. I am a truth seeker.

I graduated with a B.A. in Digital Journalism and a minor in psychology from Brigham Young University in 2017. I am currently a 2017 Teach for America Corp member in Kansas City, MO. I will be teaching Journalism and Computer Programming in the Fall.

I belong to The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter and I write about my faith from time to time as it is a huge part of my life.
